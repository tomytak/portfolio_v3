Flutter Dance Graphics

A whimsical reflection of the zoo’s spirit

The Honolulu Zoo was in a need of a logo that features its founding animals with playful imagery that speaks to its interactive, family-friendly environment.

the challenge

Honor the past while embracing the present

Redesign Honolulu Zoo’s logo to appeal to families through memorable iconography that considers, responds to, and amplifies the mission and history.

the outcome

Welcome audience participation

To emphasize the zoo’s interactive nature, the logo’s puzzle-like appearance supports their mission to create meaningful experiences to foster stewardship of the world around us.
