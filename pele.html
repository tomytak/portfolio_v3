the challenge

Inspire people to come together during social distancing.

The onset of the social distancing regulations due to COVID-19 resulted in the first entirely virtual Pele Awards from entry to the awards ceremony. How can this event boost morale and continue to draw in people to participate in this competition?

the outcome

Visualize a brighter future

The visual identity and theme focus on ways that the creative community can come together by celebrating our resiliency through the work.

Pele Awards

The Pele Awards is a long-standing competition for Hawai‘i’s creative community in the Advertising and Marketing field. Directly linked to the national ADDY awards, chosen winners will represent District 13 from our professional and college divisions.

Theme Development

theme

we shine brighter together

core attributes
  • Resiliency
  • Community
  • Opportunity

Businesses and lifestyles have changed drastically since the start of COVID- 19. We took the time to acknowledge the resiliency of the creative community this past year as a basis on which all participants can empathize. Using this point to empower people, we then focused on their combined potential to work towards a brighter future. Ultimately, the committee agreed on this theme as it demonstrates how our creative community, as a unit, is stronger as we go through trying times.
